# sales-dashboard-powerbi
📊 Sales Analytics Dashboard — Power BI
An 11-page interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyse sales performance, product trends, customer behaviour, and geographic distribution across multiple countries.

# Tools Used
Power BI Desktop
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
Data Modelling & Relationships


# Dashboard Pages Overview
PageDescriptionOverview High-level KPIs — total sales, revenue, ordersSales TrendsMonthly revenue trends over timeProduct PerformanceTop 10 / Bottom 10 products by sales & demandCustomer AnalysisCustomer count by month and segmentGeographic AnalysisCountry-level sales and order breakdownProfit vs RevenueProfit margin comparison across categoriesInventory RiskDistribution of inventory risk levels

# Key Insights
Identified Top 10 and Bottom 10 products by sales volume and demand
Tracked monthly customer growth across 5+ countries
Compared profit vs revenue to highlight high-margin product categories
Mapped geographic sales distribution to find strongest markets
